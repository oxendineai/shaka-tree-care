About Us

Meet Kaikalani: Bellingham's Tree Whisperer

Aloha! I'm Kaikalani Oxendine, the proud owner of Shaka Tree Care. My journey with trees began with a deep appreciation for their beauty and ecological importance. It's more than just a job; it's a commitment to the health of our urban canopy and the safety of our community.

  • 2 Years as a Hotshot Firefighter: My time on a Hotshot crew instilled an unparalleled discipline for safety, efficient rigging, and working effectively in challenging, high-risk tree environments. This experience is invaluable in complex tree removals and emergency situations.

  • 3 Years as a Bellingham Arborist: For the past three years, I've been hands-on with Bellingham's unique tree population, from towering cedars to delicate fruit trees. I understand the specific needs and challenges of our local flora.

At Shaka Tree Care, we combine this extensive experience with a friendly, professional approach. We're not just cutting branches; we're caring for your landscape and ensuring the longevity of your trees. "Shaka" isn't just a hand gesture; it's a philosophy of goodwill, understanding, and community that we bring to every job.
